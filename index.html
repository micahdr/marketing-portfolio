Client Demand · New Record
Inbound
Job Briefs
Q3 FY26 · Jan–Mar 2026
41
website job briefs · highest ever quarter
↑ 356% YoY  vs Q3 FY25 (9)
Employers are now coming straight to us. Dedicated job-brief form submissions (Formidable + the new Tally form) leapt from 9 in Q3 FY25 to 41 in Q3 FY26. Add the 34 hiring contact-form enquiries (also up +42% YoY) and total client-side inbound hits 75 in a single quarter (up +127%). This is the brand starting to work as a sales channel.

Note: these figures capture form submissions only. Direct calls, emails and conversations driven by brand awareness are not counted here. Inbound forms represent a fraction of total marketing impact, much of which compounds through strengthened relationships with existing contacts.
